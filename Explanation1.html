Understanding Treaty One

Today, I will talk to you about treaties, focusing on Treaty One. But first, what are treaties? A treaty, in its simplest form, is a formal, legally binding agreement between two or more nations, often governed by international law. These agreements define various aspects of relationships between nations, such as trade, security, and resource management.
